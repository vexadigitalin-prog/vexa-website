ABOUT ME

VEXA Digital was founded to address a critical gap in the Indian esports ecosystem: the absence of sustainable business architecture. Too many organizations chase sponsorships without building the foundational systems that make them sponsorship-worthy.

With experience in business strategy, revenue architecture, and the esports industry, I provide diagnostic, architectural, and supervisory services to organizations seeking to transition from funded projects to self-sustaining businesses.

My approach is direct, diagnostic, and focused on reality—not hype. I work with organizations that are ready to build structure, not just chase validation.

Operating Principle: Very Easy is Never Awesome. If building a sustainable esports business were easy, everyone would do it. The difficulty is the filter.

SERVICES

All services are designed to reduce sponsor dependency and build owned revenue pathways. Every engagement starts with consultation.
